<p><p>A Back-Illuminated Sensor (BIS) is a type of image sensor that enhances light sensitivity by positioning the photodiodes at the back of the sensor chip. These sensors are widely used in consumer electronics, particularly in smartphones and digital cameras.</p><p>The Back-Illuminated Sensor Market is expected to experience significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for high-quality imaging in smartphones, tablets, and cameras.
