<p><p>The cooking fat market is categorized into three main types: vegetable fat, animal fat, and mixed fat. Vegetable fats, derived from plant sources like oils, are popular for their health benefits and versatility in various cuisines. Animal fats, sourced from meat and dairy products, are traditional and known for their rich flavors but can be higher in saturated fats. Mixed fats combine both vegetable and animal sources, offering a balance of flavor and health attributes, catering to diverse culinary preferences.</p></p>
