<p><p>The solar cell laser equipment market consists of several key types: </p><p>1. **Laser Ablation**: Uses high-energy lasers to remove thin layers from materials, enhancing efficiency.</p><p>2. **Laser Doping**: Introduces impurities into semiconductor materials to alter electrical properties. </p><p>3. **Laser Drilling**: Creates precise holes in substrates for effective cell connections. </p><p>4. **Laser Scribing**: Enables intricate cuts on solar cells for better separation and efficiency.</p><p>5. **Laser Repairing**: Fixes defects in solar cells, prolonging their lifespan and improving performance. </p><p>These technologies optimize solar cell production and functionality.</p></p>
