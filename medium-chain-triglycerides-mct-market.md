<p><p>Medium-chain Triglycerides (MCT) are a type of fat molecule composed of fatty acids that have a chain length of 6 to 12 carbon atoms. Unlike long-chain triglycerides, MCTs are more easily and rapidly absorbed by the body, making them a popular ingredient in nutritional supplements, food products, and cosmetics. They are commonly derived from coconut oil and palm kernel oil, and are known for their potential health benefits, including weight management, improved energy levels, and enhanced cognitive function.</p><p>The Medium-chain Triglycerides (MCT) Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing consumer awareness regarding health and wellness, as well as the rising popularity of ketogenic and low-carb diets.
