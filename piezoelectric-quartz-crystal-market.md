<p><ul><li>DIP Type</li><li>SMD Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The piezoelectric quartz crystal market can be divided into two main types: DIP (Dual In-line Package) type and SMD (Surface Mount Device) type. DIP type crystals feature leads extending from both sides for through-hole mounting, commonly used in traditional electronic devices. In contrast, SMD type crystals are designed for surface mounting, allowing for higher density circuit designs and improved performance in compact applications. Both types cater to distinct needs in electronics, from consumer gadgets to industrial equipment.</p></p>
