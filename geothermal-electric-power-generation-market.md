<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Geothermal Electric Power Generation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Steam Stations</li><li>Flash Steam Power Stations</li><li>Binary Cycle Stations</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The geothermal electric power generation market consists of three main types: </p><p>1. **Dry Steam Stations** utilize steam from geothermal reservoirs directly to drive turbines, making them highly efficient.</p><p>2. **Flash Steam Power Stations** extract high-pressure hot water, which flashes to steam when pressure is reduced, powering turbines for electricity generation.</p><p>3. **Binary Cycle Stations** transfer heat from geothermal fluid to a secondary fluid with a lower boiling point, enabling electricity generation while maintaining environmental safety. Each type has distinct operational characteristics and efficiency levels.</p></p>
