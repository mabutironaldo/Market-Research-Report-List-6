<p><p>The commercial and household water purification systems market includes various types of filtration media. Activated carbon filter media effectively removes contaminants and odors. Reverse osmosis membranes offer advanced filtration by removing impurities at the molecular level. UV disinfection uses ultraviolet light to eliminate microorganisms, enhancing water safety. Other technologies may also be included.</p></p>
