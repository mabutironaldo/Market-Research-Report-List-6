<p><p>The Home and Personal Care Pigments market encompasses various types, primarily categorized into water-soluble and oil-soluble pigments. Oil-soluble pigments, on the other hand, are typically used in formulations with oils and fats, such as lotions and creams, offering durability and stability in products that require oil-based media.</p></p>
