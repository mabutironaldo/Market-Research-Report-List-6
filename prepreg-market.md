<p><p>The prepreg market consists of three primary types: glass fiber prepreg, carbon fiber prepreg, and aramid fiber prepreg. Glass fiber prepreg is widely used for its cost-effectiveness and good mechanical properties, making it suitable for construction and automotive applications. Carbon fiber prepreg offers high strength-to-weight ratios and exceptional stiffness, ideal for aerospace and high-performance sports equipment. Aramid fiber prepreg is known for its resistance to impact and heat, commonly used in protective gear and military applications, providing unique benefits across various sectors.</p></p>
