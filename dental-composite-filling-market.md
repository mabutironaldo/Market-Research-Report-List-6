<p><strong>The Dental Composite Filling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resin-based Composites</li><li>Poly-acid Modified Composites</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental composite filling market primarily consists of Resin-based Composites and Poly-acid Modified Composites. Resin-based Composites are widely used due to their excellent aesthetic properties and durability, making them suitable for anterior and posterior restorations. In contrast, Poly-acid Modified Composites, also known as glass ionomer cements, release fluoride and bond chemically to the tooth structure, making them ideal for specific applications, such as pediatric dentistry and areas at risk for decay. Together, these types cater to varying clinical needs and patient preferences.</p></p>
