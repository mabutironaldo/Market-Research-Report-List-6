<p><p>Electric power transmission transformers are critical components that facilitate the efficient transfer of electricity across various voltage levels. Low voltage transformers manage voltages up to 1 kV, commonly used in residential and commercial applications. Medium voltage transformers handle voltages between 1 kV and 35 kV, typically found in industrial settings and municipal power distribution. High voltage transformers operate above 35 kV, essential for long-distance transmission, ensuring minimal energy loss while connecting power generation to substations and end-users.</p></p>
