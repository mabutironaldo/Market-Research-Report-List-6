<p><p>The healthcare arrhythmia monitoring devices market includes various types designed for continuous heart rhythm assessment. Resting ECG devices provide a snapshot of heart activity at rest, while ECG stress test devices evaluate heart performance under physical exertion. Holter monitors offer continuous ECG recording over 24-48 hours for detailed arrhythmia analysis. Implantable cardiac monitors facilitate long-term monitoring within the body for patients with intermittent symptoms. Other devices may include external loop recorders and wearable technology for ongoing monitoring and data collection.</p></p>
