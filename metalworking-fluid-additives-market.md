<p><p>The metalworking fluid additives market consists of various types, each serving distinct roles. Lubricity agents enhance the fluid's ability to reduce friction, while emulsifiers help blend water and oil, forming stable emulsions. Corrosion inhibitors prevent rust and degradation of metal surfaces, and defoamers eliminate foam that can disrupt processes. Specialty amines act as surfactants and pH stabilizers, biocides protect against microbial growth, and other additives may include fragrances or dyes, contributing to the overall performance of metalworking fluids.</p></p>
