<p><p>Renewable polyethylene encompasses three main market types: HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene), LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene), and LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene). HDPE is known for its strength and rigidity, making it ideal for durable applications like containers and pipes. LLDPE offers flexibility and elongation, suitable for films and stretch wraps. LDPE is characterized by its softness and transparency, commonly used in plastic bags and packaging. Each type utilizes bio-based feedstocks, promoting sustainability in plastic production.</p></p>
