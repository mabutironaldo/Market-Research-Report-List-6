<p><ul><li>Natural Material Carrier</li><li>High Polymer Material Carrier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nano pharmaceutical market is characterized by two primary types of drug delivery systems: natural material carriers and high polymer material carriers. Natural material carriers utilize biocompatible substances derived from biological sources, enhancing drug stability and targeting specific cells while minimizing side effects. In contrast, high polymer material carriers are synthetic, offering customizable properties such as controlled release rates and improved drug encapsulation. Both types play crucial roles in advancing targeted therapies and improving the efficiency of drug delivery systems.</p></p>
