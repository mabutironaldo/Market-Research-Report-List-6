<p><p>The automobile horn systems market comprises primarily two types: air horns and electric horns. Air horns operate using compressed air, producing a louder, more powerful sound, making them popular for larger vehicles and emergency applications. In contrast, electric horns rely on an electric current to produce sound, offering a range of tones and are widely used in standard vehicles due to their compact size and ease of installation. Both types serve essential safety functions, alerting pedestrians and other drivers.</p></p>
