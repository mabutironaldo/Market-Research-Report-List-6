<p><p>The automotive pump market includes various types essential for vehicle performance. Oil pumps maintain lubrication in the engine, and steering pumps assist in power steering systems. Water pumps circulate coolant to regulate engine temperature, while windshield pumps provide fluid for wiper systems. Vacuum pumps support brake assist and other functions, and fuel injection pumps precisely deliver fuel to the combustion chamber.</p></p>
