Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDT) offer quick results, ideal for point-of-care testing. Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) amplifies viral genetic material, ensuring precise detection of active infections. Isothermal Nucleic Acid Amplification Technology (INAAT) provides fast and sensitive results at a constant temperature. Additional methods encompass various other techniques, contributing to a broader diagnostic landscape for hepatitis.</p></p>
