<p><ul><li>Free Lutein</li><li>Lutein Esters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lutein market is primarily categorized into two types: free lutein and lutein esters. Free lutein is the naturally occurring form, often derived from marigold flowers, and is known for its high bioavailability, making it effective for ocular health and dietary supplements. Lutein esters, on the other hand, are modified forms of lutein, typically in a fatty acid ester form that enhances stability and absorption. Both types cater to various applications in nutraceuticals, cosmetics, and food industries.</p></p>
