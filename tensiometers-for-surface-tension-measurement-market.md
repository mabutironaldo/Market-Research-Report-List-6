<p><p>Tensiometers are instruments used to measure surface tension in liquids, crucial in various industrial and research applications. The market is divided into static and dynamic tensiometers. Static tensiometers assess surface tension at equilibrium, often using methods like the drop weight or pendant drop techniques. Dynamic tensiometers, on the other hand, measure tension changes during liquid manipulation, employing techniques such as maximum bubble pressure or Wilhelmy plate methods. Both types provide essential data for developing products and processes across diverse sectors.</p></p>
