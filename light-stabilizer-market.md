<p><p>The light stabilizer market comprises several types, each serving specific protective functions. Light blockers absorb harmful light, preventing it from damaging materials. UV absorbers capture ultraviolet radiation, reducing degradation and discoloration. Quenchers act to dissipate excited state energy, minimizing photodegradation. Free radical scavengers neutralize reactive species generated by light exposure, enhancing durability. Other market segments may include emerging technologies and novel compounds that address specific application needs, further diversifying the protective solutions available for various industries.</p></p>
