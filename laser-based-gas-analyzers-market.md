<p><ul><li>Tunable Diode Laser Absorption Spectroscopy</li><li>Raman Analyzers</li><li>Cavity-Ring Down Spectroscopy</li><li>Quantum Cascade Laser Spectroscopy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laser-based gas analyzers encompass various technologies for detecting and quantifying gases. Tunable Diode Laser Absorption Spectroscopy (TDLAS) offers high sensitivity and selectivity for specific gas molecules. Raman analyzers utilize inelastic scattering for molecular identification, beneficial in complex mixtures. Cavity-Ring Down Spectroscopy (CRDS) enhances detection limits by measuring light decay in a cavity. Quantum Cascade Laser Spectroscopy (QCLS) utilizes mid-infrared wavelengths for real-time monitoring of trace gases, providing versatile applications across environmental, industrial, and medical fields.</p></p>
