<p><strong>The Polyurethane Sealant for Automobile Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Component</li><li>Multi Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyurethane sealants in the automobile market are categorized into single-component and multi-component types. Single-component sealants are ready-to-use and cure upon exposure to moisture, making them convenient for quick applications. In contrast, multi-component sealants require the mixing of two or more components, often resulting in enhanced performance, including superior adhesion and flexibility. Both types are widely used for sealing joints, adhering components, and providing protection against moisture, ensuring durability and longevity in automotive applications.</p></p>
