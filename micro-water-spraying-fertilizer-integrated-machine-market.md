<p><p>The Micro Water Spraying Fertilizer Integrated Machine market primarily features two types: Film Irrigation and Under Film Irrigation. Film Irrigation involves applying a thin layer of plastic film on the soil surface, enhancing water and nutrient retention while minimizing evaporation. This method aids in consistent moisture supply. Under Film Irrigation takes it a step further by placing irrigation tubes beneath the film, enabling precise water and fertilizer delivery directly to plant roots, improving efficiency and crop yield.</p></p>
