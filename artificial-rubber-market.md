<p><p>The artificial rubber market comprises several types, each with unique properties and applications. Styrene Butadiene Rubber (SBR) is widely used in tires and footwear due to its excellent abrasion resistance. Polybutadiene (BR) offers superior resilience and is commonly found in tires. Nitrile Butadiene Rubber (NBR) is known for its oil resistance, making it ideal for automotive seals. Ethylene-Propylenediene Rubber (EPDM) is favored for weather-resistant products. Chloroprene Rubber (CR) provides good chemical stability, while "Others" includes various niche rubbers with specialized uses.</p></p>
