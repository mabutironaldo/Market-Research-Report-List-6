<p><ul><li>Ball Contact Bearings</li><li>Roller Contact Bearings</li><li>Needle Contact Bearings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Contact bearings are classified into three main types based on their rolling elements: ball, roller, and needle contact bearings. Ball contact bearings use spherical balls for reduced friction and high-speed performance, suitable for various applications. Roller contact bearings use cylindrical rollers for better load distribution, ideal for heavy-duty applications. Needle contact bearings, characterized by their elongated rollers, provide a compact design with high load capacity, often used in confined spaces. Each type serves distinct operational needs in machinery and equipment.</p></p>
