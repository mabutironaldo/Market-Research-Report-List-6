Airships are large, lighter-than-air vehicles suitable for long-duration missions and can carry significant payloads. Tethered aerostat systems utilize helium-filled balloons tethered to the ground, offering stable platforms for various applications like monitoring and communication. Each type serves distinct roles in aerial operations, focusing on efficiency and coverage.</p></p>
