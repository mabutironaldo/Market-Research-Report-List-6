<p><strong>The Elevator Cables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TVVB</li><li>RVV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The elevator cables market primarily includes two key types: TVVB and RVV cables. TVVB cables are designed for vertical applications, featuring flexible copper conductors with a PVC outer sheath, making them suitable for elevators and escalators. RVV cables, on the other hand, are multi-core and flexible, commonly used for general wiring in elevators, offering improved flexibility and durability. Both cable types ensure reliable performance and safety in vertical transport systems, catering to varying installation requirements and specifications.</p></p>
