<p><p>Concrete Weight Coatings (CWC) are essential for providing ballast and protecting underwater pipelines. The market includes several methods of application: </p><p>1. **Jet Impact Method** applies concrete using high-pressure jets for precise placement.</p><p>2. **Extrusion Winding Method** involves winding concrete around the pipe in a continuous process for uniform coating.</p><p>3. **Sliding Mode Method** employs a sliding template to achieve an even layer.</p><p>4. **Centrifugal Grouting Method** uses centrifugal force to distribute concrete evenly, ensuring robust covering. </p><p>These methods cater to various operational needs and project specifications.</p></p>
