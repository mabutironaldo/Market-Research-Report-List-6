<p><p>The Automated Turf Harvester market is primarily categorized into two types: Roll Turf Harvesters and Slab Turf Harvesters. Roll Turf Harvesters efficiently cut and roll grass into manageable rolls for easy transport and installation, ideal for large agricultural applications. In contrast, Slab Turf Harvesters slice turf into thicker slabs, typically for commercial landscaping projects where a more substantial piece of turf is necessary. Both types enhance labor efficiency and improve the quality of turf installation processes.</p></p>
