<p><strong>The Automotive TPMS Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct TPMS</li><li>Indirect TPMS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive TPMS (Tire Pressure Monitoring System) market comprises two main types: Direct TPMS and Indirect TPMS. Direct TPMS utilizes pressure sensors installed within each tire to provide real-time pressure readings, enhancing accuracy and safety. In contrast, Indirect TPMS relies on the vehicle's ABS and wheel speed sensors to estimate tire pressure based on rotational dynamics, making it less precise but more cost-effective. Both systems aim to improve vehicle safety by alerting drivers to under-inflated tires.</p></p>
