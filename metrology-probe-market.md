<p><ul><li>Machine Tool Probe</li><li>CMM Probe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metrology probe market encompasses various types, including machine tool probes and coordinate measuring machine (CMM) probes. Machine tool probes enhance precision during manufacturing processes by detecting workpiece dimensions in real-time, ensuring quality control and reducing scrap. CMM probes, on the other hand, are used in coordinate measuring machines to capture geometrical data of objects, allowing for detailed inspection and verification against design specifications. Both types play crucial roles in maintaining accuracy and efficiency in manufacturing and inspection processes.</p></p>
