<p><strong>The Polycarbonate (PC) Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Phosgene</li><li>Non-Phosgene</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polycarbonate (PC) resin market is primarily segmented into two types: phosgene and non-phosgene methods. The phosgene process utilizes carbon monoxide and chlorine to produce polycarbonate, offering high efficiency and purity. Conversely, the non-phosgene method often employs alternative raw materials, such as methylene diphenyl diisocyanate (MDI), to manufacture PC resins with a focus on reducing toxic emissions and environmental impact. Each method caters to diverse industrial needs, influencing applications in industries like automotive, electronics, and construction.</p></p>
