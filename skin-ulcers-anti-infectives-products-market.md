<p><p>The skin ulcers anti-infectives products market comprises several types, addressing specific conditions: pressure ulcers, diabetic ulcers, venous ulcers, and arterial ulcers. Pressure ulcers occur due to prolonged pressure on the skin, mainly in immobile patients. Diabetic ulcers result from neuropathy and poor circulation in diabetes. Venous ulcers develop from improper blood flow, often in the lower legs, while arterial ulcers stem from inadequate blood supply. Each type requires tailored anti-infective treatments to promote healing and prevent infections.</p></p>
