<p><p>The ocular drug delivery system market includes implantable ocular drug delivery systems and particulate drug delivery systems. Implantable systems are devices inserted into the eye for long-term administration of therapeutic agents, enhancing precision and reducing systemic exposure. Particulate drug delivery systems utilize nanoparticles or microspheres to encapsulate drugs, allowing for controlled release and improved bioavailability. Together, these technologies aim to enhance the effectiveness of eye treatments while minimizing side effects and improving patient compliance in managing ocular diseases.</p></p>
