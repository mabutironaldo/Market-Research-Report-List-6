<p><p>The airway stent market primarily consists of tracheal stents and laryngeal stents. Tracheal stents are designed to maintain open airways in patients with tracheal obstructions, facilitating easier breathing and ventilation. Laryngeal stents, on the other hand, are used to support the larynx and prevent collapse, commonly in cases of laryngeal tumors or trauma. Both types are crucial in managing airway patency, improving patient outcomes, and enhancing quality of life in individuals with respiratory conditions.</p></p>
