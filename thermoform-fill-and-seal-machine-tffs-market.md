The market for TFFS machines is divided into fully automatic and semi-automatic types. Fully automatic machines are designed for high-volume production with minimal manual intervention, enhancing efficiency and consistency. Semi-automatic machines require some manual input, making them suitable for smaller operations or businesses that need flexibility, offering a balance between cost and productivity.</p></p>
