<p><strong>The Handheld Thermal Coagulator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unipolar Coagulator</li><li>Multipolar Coagulator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld thermal coagulator market includes two primary types: unipolar coagulators and multipolar coagulators. Unipolar coagulators use a single electrode to deliver current through the tissue, effectively coagulating blood vessels and facilitating surgical procedures. In contrast, multipolar coagulators utilize multiple electrodes to create a more localized and controlled coagulation effect, minimizing thermal spread and improving precision. Both types are essential in various surgical applications, enhancing patient outcomes through effective hemostasis and tissue management.</p></p>
