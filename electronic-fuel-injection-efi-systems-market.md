<p><ul><li>Single-point Injection Systems</li><li>Multi-point Injection Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electronic Fuel Injection (EFI) Systems market is primarily categorized into two types: Single-point Injection Systems and Multi-point Injection Systems. Single-point injection utilizes one injector to distribute fuel to all cylinders, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, often found in older or smaller engines. Multi-point injection, on the other hand, employs individual injectors for each cylinder, enhancing fuel efficiency, performance, and emissions control, commonly seen in modern vehicles. This market segment is driven by advancements in technology and increasing environmental regulations.</p></p>
