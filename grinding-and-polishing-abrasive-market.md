<p><p>The grinding and polishing abrasive market comprises three main types: bonded abrasives, coated abrasives, and super abrasives. Bonded abrasives are composite materials that contain abrasive grains held together with a binding agent, ideal for heavy-duty applications. Coated abrasives consist of abrasive grains adhered to a flexible backing, suitable for versatile surface finishing tasks. Super abrasives, such as diamond and CBN, offer superior cutting efficiency and durability, primarily for precision machining and hard materials. Together, they enhance surface quality in various industries.</p></p>
