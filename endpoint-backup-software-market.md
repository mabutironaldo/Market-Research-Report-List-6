<p><p>Endpoint backup software is available in two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based endpoint backup software stores data on remote servers managed by a third-party provider, offering flexibility, scalability, and accessibility from anywhere with an internet connection. On the other hand, on-premises endpoint backup software is installed and managed on local servers, providing greater control and security over data storage and management. Each market type caters to different needs and preferences of businesses based on their requirements and infrastructure.</p></p>
