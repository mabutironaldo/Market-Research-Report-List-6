Non-Reinforced membranes are flexible and provide excellent barrier properties, ideal for sensitive environments. Titanium Reinforced membranes combine the durability of titanium with PTFE, enhancing mechanical strength and chemical resistance, which is essential for demanding industrial applications. Each type caters to distinct operational needs and environments.</p></p>
