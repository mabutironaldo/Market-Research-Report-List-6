<p><ul><li>Passive Electronic Filter</li><li>Active Electronic Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-pass electronic filter market is divided into two primary types: passive and active electronic filters. Passive electronic filters use reactive components, such as capacitors and inductors, to allow high-frequency signals while blocking lower frequencies, requiring no external power source. In contrast, active electronic filters incorporate amplifying components like operational amplifiers, enabling better performance and control over the frequency response. Both types serve various applications in audio processing, telecommunications, and signal conditioning, catering to diverse engineering needs.</p></p>
