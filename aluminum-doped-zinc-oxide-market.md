<p><p>The aluminum-doped zinc oxide (AZO) market encompasses various production methods, including the Sol-Gel method and Pulsed Laser Deposition (PLD). The Sol-Gel method involves transitioning from a liquid solution to a solid material, allowing precise control over composition and morphology, making it suitable for coatings and thin films. Pulsed Laser Deposition, on the other hand, utilizes high-energy laser pulses to deposit AZO films with high purity and density. Other methods may include sputtering and chemical vapor deposition, catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
