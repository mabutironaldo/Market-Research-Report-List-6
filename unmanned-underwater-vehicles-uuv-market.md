<p><ul><li>Remotely Operated Vehicle (Rov)</li><li>Autonomous Underwater Vehicle (Auv)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Unmanned Underwater Vehicles (UUVs) encompass two primary market types: Remotely Operated Vehicles (ROVs) and Autonomous Underwater Vehicles (AUVs). ROVs are tethered vehicles controlled by operators on the surface, often used for tasks requiring real-time human intervention, such as inspections and repairs. AUVs, in contrast, operate independently, following pre-programmed paths for data collection, mapping, or environmental monitoring. Both types are essential in marine research, oil and gas exploration, and defense operations, contributing to advancements in underwater technology and exploration.</p></p>
