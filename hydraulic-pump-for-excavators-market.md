<p><strong>The Hydraulic Pump for Excavators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gear Pump</li><li>Piston Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic pump market for excavators primarily features two types: gear pumps and piston pumps. Gear pumps utilize interlocking gears to create fluid movement, offering high efficiency and compact design, ideal for low-pressure applications. In contrast, piston pumps employ cylindrical pistons to generate higher pressure, making them suitable for heavy-duty excavation tasks. Both types cater to different operational needs, with gear pumps favored for their simplicity and cost-effectiveness, while piston pumps excel in performance under demanding conditions.</p></p>
