<p><ul><li>Matched Connectors/Seals</li><li>Compression Connectors/Seals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Glass-to-Metal Connectors market is primarily divided into two types: Matched Connectors/Seals and Compression Connectors/Seals. Matched Connectors/Seals are designed for precise alignment and minimal thermal and mechanical stress, ensuring reliable electrical connections in sensitive applications such as aerospace and medical devices. Compression Connectors/Seals, on the other hand, utilize mechanical force to create a secure bond between glass and metal, often used in high-pressure environments, enhancing durability and performance in industrial applications. Both types are essential for achieving optimal sealing and conductivity.</p></p>
