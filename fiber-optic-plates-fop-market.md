<p><p>Fiber Optic Plates (FOP) are crucial components in optical systems, available in two primary types: straight and tapered. Straight FOPs provide uniform light transmission, making them ideal for applications where consistent image quality is essential, such as in imaging systems. Tapered FOPs, on the other hand, offer a reduction in size and are used to enhance resolution by concentrating light across a smaller area. Each type serves specific applications in fields like medical imaging, industrial inspection, and scientific research.</p></p>
