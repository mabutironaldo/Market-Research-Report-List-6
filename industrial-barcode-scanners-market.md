<p><strong>The Industrial Barcode Scanners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser Scanners</li><li>2D Imagers</li><li>Linear Imagers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial barcode scanners market encompasses three main types: laser scanners, 2D imagers, and linear imagers. Laser scanners use laser beams to capture and decode barcodes with high speed and accuracy, ideal for long-range scanning. 2D imagers utilize camera technology to read both 1D and 2D barcodes, allowing for versatile applications and the ability to capture images. Linear imagers, while similar to laser scanners, rely on a linear array of sensors to read barcodes, offering a balance between speed and cost.</p></p>
