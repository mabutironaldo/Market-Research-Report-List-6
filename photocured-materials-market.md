<p><strong>The Photocured Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Free-radical Type Photoinitiator</li><li>Cationic Type Photoinitiator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photocured materials market encompasses two primary types of photoinitiators: free-radical and cationic. Free-radical photoinitiators generate free radicals upon exposure to light, facilitating polymerization in applications like coatings and adhesives. These are widely used due to their fast curing times and versatility. In contrast, cationic photoinitiators initiate polymerization through cationic reactions, offering benefits such as resistance to oxygen inhibition and improved thermal stability. Both types cater to various industries, including automotive, electronics, and dental applications, enhancing product performance.</p></p>
