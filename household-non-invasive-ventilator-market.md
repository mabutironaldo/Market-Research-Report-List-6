<p><p>The household non-invasive ventilator market includes three main types: CPAP, BiPAP, and APAP. CPAP (Continuous Positive Airway Pressure) provides a constant airflow to keep airways open, primarily for sleep apnea treatment. BiPAP (Bilevel Positive Airway Pressure) offers two pressure levels for inhalation and exhalation, catering to patients with more complex respiratory issues. APAP (Auto-titrating Positive Airway Pressure) automatically adjusts pressure settings based on patient needs throughout the night, enhancing comfort and effectiveness. Each type serves specific respiratory support requirements.</p></p>
