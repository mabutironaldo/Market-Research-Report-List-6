<p><p>An infrared gas sensor is a device that detects the presence and concentration of various gases in the environment using infrared light. These sensors are particularly effective for gases like carbon dioxide, methane, and volatile organic compounds (VOCs), as they absorb specific wavelengths of infrared light. When the gas concentration increases, the amount of infrared light reaching the sensor decreases, which then generates a signal indicative of the gas concentration.</p><p>The Infrared Gas Sensor Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 0.67% during the forecast period.
