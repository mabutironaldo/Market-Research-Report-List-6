<p><ul><li>Energy Capacitors</li><li>Power Capacitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Supercapacitors for the automotive market can be categorized into two main types: energy capacitors and power capacitors. Energy capacitors are designed to store and release large amounts of energy over a longer period of time, making them ideal for applications such as regenerative braking. On the other hand, power capacitors are designed to deliver high bursts of power quickly, making them suitable for applications like starting the engine or providing additional acceleration. Both types of supercapacitors play an important role in enhancing the efficiency and performance of automotive systems.</p></p>
