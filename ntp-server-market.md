<p><p>NTP (Network Time Protocol) servers are categorized by their ingress protection (IP) ratings, which indicate environmental resistance. IP65-rated NTP servers are designed for harsh conditions, offering complete dust protection and resistance to water jets, making them suitable for outdoor or industrial settings. IP30-rated servers provide moderate protection against solid objects but are not water-resistant, ideal for indoor environments. IP20-rated servers offer minimal protection, primarily safeguarding against large foreign objects, making them suitable for controlled indoor use. Each type serves specific operational needs.</p></p>
