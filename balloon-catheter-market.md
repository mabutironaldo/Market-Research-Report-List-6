<p><strong>The Balloon Catheter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normal Balloon Catheter</li><li>Cutting Balloon Catheter</li><li>Scoring Balloon Catheter</li><li>Drug Eluting Balloon Catheter</li><li>Stent Graft Balloon Catheter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The balloon catheter market consists of various types designed for specific medical applications. Normal balloon catheters inflate to dilate narrowed vessels, while cutting balloon catheters feature micro-blades to enhance lesion modification. Scoring balloon catheters utilize longitudinal scores to aid vessel expansion without excessive trauma. Drug-eluting balloon catheters release medication to prevent restenosis post-procedure. Stent graft balloon catheters help deploy stent grafts, ensuring effective reinforcement of blood vessels. Each type serves critical roles in interventional procedures, enhancing patient outcomes.</p></p>
