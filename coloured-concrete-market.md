<p><p>The coloured concrete market is divided into two main types: synthetic and natural. Synthetic coloured concrete utilizes pigments or dyes manufactured from chemicals, allowing for a wide range of vivid colors and consistent results. It is often used for decorative purposes in residential and commercial applications. In contrast, natural coloured concrete derives its hues from naturally occurring minerals or aggregates, offering earthy tones and a more organic appearance. This type is often preferred for environmentally conscious projects, blending seamlessly with landscapes.</p></p>
