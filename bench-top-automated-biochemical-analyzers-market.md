<p><p>Bench-top automated biochemical analyzers can be categorized into two types: semi-automated and fully-automated. Semi-automated analyzers require some manual intervention from operators, such as sample preparation and loading. On the other hand, fully-automated analyzers carry out the entire analysis process without the need for human involvement, from sample loading to result generation. Fully-automated analyzers are preferred for their efficiency and accuracy, while semi-automated analyzers offer more flexibility and control to operators. Both types cater to different laboratory requirements and preferences.</p></p>
