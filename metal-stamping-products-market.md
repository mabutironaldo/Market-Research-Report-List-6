<p><p>The metal stamping products market encompasses various techniques used to shape and manipulate metal sheets. The blanking process involves cutting out flat shapes, while embossing creates raised designs. The bending process involves altering the metal's angle or shape, and coining precisely shapes or compresses metal for fine details. Flanging adds a tab or rim to the edges for strength or design purposes. Each method serves distinct applications across industries such as automotive, consumer goods, and aerospace.</p></p>
