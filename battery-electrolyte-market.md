<p><ul><li>Liquid Electrolyte</li><li>Gel Electrolyte</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The battery electrolyte market is primarily categorized into liquid and gel electrolytes. Liquid electrolytes, typically composed of solvents and salts, facilitate efficient ion transport, making them ideal for conventional lithium-ion and lead-acid batteries. In contrast, gel electrolytes, which combine liquid electrolytes with a gelling agent, offer enhanced safety and stability, reducing the risk of leakage and evaporation. These properties make gel electrolytes suitable for applications requiring a balance of performance and reliability, such as in electric vehicles and renewable energy storage.</p></p>
