<p><p>The Positron Emission Computed Tomography (PET) market can be categorized into three types: Direct Imaging, Indirect Imaging, and Alternative Imaging. Direct Imaging involves capturing images from emitted positrons directly, providing high-resolution data. Indirect Imaging uses a scintillation detector to convert emitted radiation into visible light, which is then transformed into images. Alternative Imaging includes newer technologies like combined PET/MRI scans, enhancing diagnostic capabilities by integrating different imaging modalities, improving accuracy and providing comprehensive patient assessments.</p></p>
