<p><ul><li>Inflatable Seat Belt</li><li>Common Belt</li><li>Single-stage Belt Force Limiters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive seat belt market features various types designed for enhanced safety and comfort. Inflatable seat belts deploy an airbag-like feature, cushioning occupants during crashes. Common belts are the standard three-point systems using retractable straps to secure passengers. Single-stage belt force limiters reduce the force applied on occupants in a collision by allowing some elongation of the belt. Together, these innovations improve occupant protection and contribute to advancements in vehicle safety technology.</p></p>
