<p><strong>The Single Crystal Silicon Carbide Substrate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conductive Substrate</li><li>Semi-insulating Substrate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Single Crystal Silicon Carbide substrate market is primarily divided into two types: conductive substrates and semi-insulating substrates. Conductive substrates have low resistivity, enabling efficient charge transport, making them ideal for high-power and high-frequency applications. In contrast, semi-insulating substrates possess high resistivity, reducing unwanted charge carrier levels, and are often used in electronic and optoelectronic devices to minimize losses and ensure performance stability. These substrate types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, aerospace, and telecommunications.</p></p>
