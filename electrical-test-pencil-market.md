<p><p>The electrical test pencil market features two primary types: contact type test pencils and induction type test pencils. Contact type test pencils directly measure voltage by making physical contact with a conductor, providing accurate readings for electrical circuits. In contrast, induction type test pencils detect voltage without direct contact, using electromagnetic fields to sense the presence of electrical charge, making them safer for quick assessments. Both types serve essential roles in ensuring electrical safety and facilitating efficient troubleshooting.</p></p>
