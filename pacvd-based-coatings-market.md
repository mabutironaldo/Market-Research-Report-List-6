<p><p>PACVD, or Plasma-Assisted Chemical Vapor Deposition, features several market types including RF, DC, and Microwave PACVD. RF PACVD utilizes radio frequency energy to generate plasma, allowing for uniform coatings on various substrates. DC PACVD relies on direct current for plasma generation, typically offering faster deposition rates. Microwave PACVD employs microwave energy, facilitating exceptional control over the deposition process and producing high-quality coatings with minimal defects. Together, these technologies cater to diverse industrial applications, enhancing surface properties and performance.</p></p>
