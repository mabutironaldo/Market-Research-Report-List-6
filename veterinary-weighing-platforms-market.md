<p><p>Veterinary weighing platforms are available in two main types: electronic and mechanical. Mechanical platforms, on the other hand, rely on traditional weighing mechanisms such as springs or levers to measure weight. While they may be more cost-effective, they may not provide as accurate or consistent readings as electronic platforms.
