<p><p>Pipeline pigging systems are crucial for maintaining pipeline integrity and efficiency. Ultrasonic Pigging (UT) utilizes sound waves to detect corrosion and defects, providing precise thickness measurements. Magnetic Flux Leakage (MFL) employs magnetic fields to identify flaws in the pipeline wall by measuring the leakage of magnetic flux. Other market types may include traditional mechanical pigs and robotic inspection tools, each serving distinct purposes in cleaning, inspecting, and maintaining pipelines to ensure safety and operational efficiency.</p></p>
