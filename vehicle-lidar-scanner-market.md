<p><ul><li>Mechanical Lidar</li><li>Solid State Lidar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vehicle LiDAR Scanner market comprises two main types: Mechanical LiDAR and Solid-State LiDAR. Mechanical LiDAR uses rotating components to emit and receive laser beams, offering high resolution and long-range capabilities, making it suitable for autonomous vehicles and advanced mapping. Solid-State LiDAR, on the other hand, has no moving parts, relying on fixed sensors for laser emission and detection. It is more compact, durable, and cost-effective, ideal for mass-market automotive applications and enhanced safety systems. Both types are pivotal in advancing autonomous driving and vehicle safety technologies.</p></p>
