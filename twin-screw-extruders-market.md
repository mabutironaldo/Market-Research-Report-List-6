<p><ul><li>Co-Rotating</li><li>Counter Rotating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Twin-screw extruders are critical in the processing of polymers and other materials, categorized primarily into co-rotating and counter-rotating types. Co-rotating extruders feature screws that rotate in the same direction, enhancing material mixing, conveying, and plasticization, ideal for applications requiring uniform dispersion. In contrast, counter-rotating extruders have screws that rotate in opposite directions, offering tighter control over material flow and shear rates, making them suitable for applications needing precise processing. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
