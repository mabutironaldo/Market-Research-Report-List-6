<p><p>The Magnetic Resonance Image-Guided Focused Ultrasound (MRgFUS) system market encompasses three primary device types: extracorporeal, intracavitary, and interstitial devices. Extracorporeal devices operate outside the body, directing ultrasound waves through soft tissue to target areas non-invasively. Intracavitary devices are inserted into natural body cavities to deliver focused ultrasound treatments directly to internal structures. Interstitial devices are implanted within tissues to provide targeted thermal ablation, effectively treating conditions like tumors or fibroids while minimizing damage to surrounding tissues.</p></p>
