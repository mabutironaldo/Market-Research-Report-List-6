<p><p>The aluminum foil shielding tape market is categorized into one-sided and double-sided types. One-sided tapes feature adhesive only on one side, making them ideal for applications where a clean surface is needed, such as electrical insulation or electromagnetic interference (EMI) shielding. In contrast, double-sided tapes have adhesive on both sides, enabling secure bonding between surfaces, which is beneficial for complex applications like cable connections or multi-layer constructions. Both types serve essential roles in various industrial and consumer electronics sectors.</p></p>
