<p><p>PTC heaters for household appliances include three main types: PTC air heaters, PTC heat conductors, and convection heaters. PTC air heaters utilize positive temperature coefficient materials to efficiently heat air for space heating. PTC heat conductors transfer heat directly to surfaces or objects, providing localized warmth. Convection heaters rely on natural or forced airflow to distribute warmth evenly in a room. All three types offer energy efficiency and rapid heating capabilities, making them popular choices for modern households.</p></p>
