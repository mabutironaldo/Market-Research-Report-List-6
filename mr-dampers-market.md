<p><p>The MR dampers market is categorized into two primary types: active dampers and semi-active dampers. Active dampers utilize external power sources to adjust their force output in real time, providing enhanced control over vehicle dynamics and ride comfort. In contrast, semi-active dampers adjust their damping characteristics based on changing conditions, using a controlled mechanism without a power supply. Both types are crucial for improving vehicle stability and passenger comfort, serving various applications in automotive and industrial sectors.</p></p>
