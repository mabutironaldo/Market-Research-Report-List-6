<p><p>The coloring foodstuff market is categorized into liquid and powder types. Liquid colorants are often used for beverages, sauces, and baked goods, providing vibrant hues and ease of mixing. They offer versatility and quick absorption, making them popular in commercial food production. Powdered colorants, on the other hand, are concentrated and often used in dry mixes, snacks, and confectionery items. They have a longer shelf life and are ideal for products requiring precise measurements of color while minimizing moisture content.</p></p>
