<p><p>The man-made sausage casing market encompasses several types, each with unique properties. Collagen casings are derived from animal skins and offer a uniform texture and strength. Cellulose casings, made from plant fibers, are non-edible and commonly used for skinless sausages. Plastic casings provide excellent barrier properties and are often utilized for dry or pre-cooked products. Additionally, "others" include casings from various synthetic materials that cater to specific processing needs and product characteristics, enhancing overall versatility in sausage production.</p></p>
