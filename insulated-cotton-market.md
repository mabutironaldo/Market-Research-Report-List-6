<p><p>The insulated cotton market includes Ceramic Fiber Cotton, Rock Cotton, and Glass Fiber Cotton, each serving distinct thermal insulation needs. Ceramic Fiber Cotton, made from alumina-silica, excels in high-temperature applications like furnaces. Rock Cotton, derived from basalt, offers fire resistance and soundproofing, ideal for construction. Glass Fiber Cotton, composed of fine glass fibers, provides lightweight insulation for HVAC systems and industrial equipment. All three types prioritize energy efficiency, durability, and safety, catering to diverse industries such as manufacturing, construction, and automotive.</p></p>
