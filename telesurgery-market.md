<p><p>Telesurgery, also known as remote surgery or robotic surgery, is a surgical procedure performed by a surgeon at a location distant from the patient using robotic or computer-assisted technology. The key drivers for market growth include advancements in robotic technology, increasing demand for minimally invasive surgeries, and a growing global geriatric population. Additionally, the COVID-19 pandemic has accelerated the adoption of telesurgery as a way to reduce the risk of infection for both patients and medical staff.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the Telesurgery Market is the development of teleoperation systems that allow for haptic feedback, which provides the surgeon with a sense of touch during the procedure. This technology is enhancing the precision and safety of telesurgery procedures, leading to better patient outcomes and driving the market growth further.</p></p>
