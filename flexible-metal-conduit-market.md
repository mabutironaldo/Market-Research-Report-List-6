<p><p>Flexible Metal Conduit (FMC) is a type of electrical conduit made from spirally wound metal, designed to protect and route electrical wiring in various applications. FMC offers improved flexibility compared to rigid conduits, making it suitable for installations in tight spaces and environments requiring movement or vibration absorption. Its robustness against moisture, chemicals, and temperature fluctuations enhances its utility across industries such as construction, manufacturing, and transportation.</p><p>The Flexible Metal Conduit Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.3% during the forecast period. Recent trends include the development of environmentally friendly FMC solutions and advancements in manufacturing techniques that improve product performance. Innovations in connectivity and material technology are also enhancing the adaptability and longevity of these conduits, further solidifying their role in modern electrical installations.
