<p><p>The moisture barrier fabric market comprises two main types: breathable and non-breathable fabrics. Breathable moisture barrier fabrics allow water vapor to escape while preventing liquid water from penetrating, making them ideal for garments and outdoor gear where comfort and moisture management are essential. Non-breathable moisture barrier fabrics, on the other hand, offer complete protection against water ingress but can trap moisture and heat, making them suitable for applications like industrial safety gear and waterproof construction materials where durability is prioritized.</p></p>
