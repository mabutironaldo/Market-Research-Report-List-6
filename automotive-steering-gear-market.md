<p><p>The Automotive Steering Gear Market can be categorized into Type I and Type II markets based on the steering mechanisms employed. Type I typically includes conventional mechanical systems such as rack and pinion steering, offering direct feedback and simplicity. In contrast, Type II encompasses advanced systems like electric power steering (EPS) or steer-by-wire technologies, providing enhanced control, energy efficiency, and adaptability. Both types aim to improve vehicle handling and driver comfort while addressing safety and performance requirements in modern automobiles.</p></p>
