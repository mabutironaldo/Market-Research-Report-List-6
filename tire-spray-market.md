<p><p>The tire spray market is primarily divided into two types: water-based tire sprays and silicone-based tire sprays. Water-based tire sprays are eco-friendly, offering a low environmental impact while providing a matte finish and reasonable protection. They are easier to clean and apply but may require more frequent application. In contrast, silicone-based tire sprays deliver a high-gloss finish and superior durability against UV rays and weathering. However, they can attract dirt and may have a longer drying time. Both types serve distinct consumer preferences and needs in tire maintenance.</p></p>
