Liquid saturated polyester resins are typically used for coatings, adhesives, and composites due to their excellent flow properties and ease of application. In contrast, solid saturated polyester resins are more suitable for applications requiring higher mechanical strength and durability, such as in thermoplastic materials and molded products. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, including automotive, construction, and electronics.</p></p>
