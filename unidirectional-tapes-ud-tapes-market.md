<p><p>Unidirectional Tapes (UD Tapes) are advanced composite materials featuring continuous fibers aligned in a single direction, enhancing strength and stiffness. They are categorized into two main types: thermoplastic UD tapes, which can be reshaped upon heating, offering recyclability and processing flexibility; and thermoset UD tapes, which cure into a rigid form, providing excellent heat and chemical resistance. Both types are widely used in aerospace, automotive, and sports equipment industries for lightweight, high-performance applications.</p></p>
