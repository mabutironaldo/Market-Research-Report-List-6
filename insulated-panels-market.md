<p><strong>The Insulated Panels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>EPS Panels</li><li>PUR/PIR Panels</li><li>Mineral/Glass Wool Panels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The insulated panels market comprises three primary types: EPS panels, PUR/PIR panels, and mineral/glass wool panels. EPS (expanded polystyrene) panels are lightweight and offer good thermal insulation, making them popular for construction. PUR (polyurethane) and PIR (polyisocyanurate) panels provide superior thermal performance and moisture resistance, ideal for energy-efficient buildings. Mineral and glass wool panels are known for their excellent fire resistance and sound insulation properties, commonly used in industrial and commercial applications. Each type addresses specific insulation needs.</p></p>
