<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Security CMOS Image Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FSI</li><li>BSI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Security CMOS Image Sensor Market can be divided into two types: Front Side Illumination (FSI) and Back Side Illumination (BSI). </p><p>FSI sensors have a traditional design where the photodiode array is located on top of the silicon substrate, while BSI sensors have the photodiode array positioned below the silicon substrate, allowing for better light sensitivity. BSI sensors are considered to be more advanced and efficient compared to FSI sensors, making them a popular choice for security applications requiring high-quality image capture in low-light conditions.</p></p>
