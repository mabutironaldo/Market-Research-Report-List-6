<p><strong>The Thermoplastic Purging Compound Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Purging</li><li>Chemical/Foaming Purging</li><li>Liquid Purging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermoplastic purging compound market is categorized into three main types: mechanical purging, chemical/foaming purging, and liquid purging. Mechanical purging involves the use of solid materials to physically remove leftover thermoplastics from machinery. Chemical/foaming purging employs reactive agents that create foam, enhancing the cleaning process by displacing residues. Liquid purging utilizes liquid purging agents designed to dissolve or flush out clogs and residues, ensuring a thorough cleaning of equipment, enhancing production efficiency, and reducing downtime.</p></p>
