<p><p>The Chip-on-Board (CoB) LED market consists of three primary types: lateral chip, vertical chip, and flip chip. Lateral chip LEDs feature a horizontal layout that provides wide light distribution, ideal for general lighting. Vertical chip LEDs, designed in a vertical alignment, optimize thermal management and enhance light output, making them suitable for compact applications. Flip chip LEDs utilize an upward mounting approach that facilitates better thermal conductivity and can achieve higher efficiency, catering to high-performance lighting needs.</p></p>
