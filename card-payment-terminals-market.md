<p><p>The card payment terminals market is categorized into three main types: countertop card machines, portable card machines, and mobile card machines. Countertop machines are fixed terminals primarily used in retail locations, providing stable connectivity for transactions. Portable machines allow merchants to move freely within a store, enhancing customer service by facilitating payments anywhere on the premises. Mobile card machines enable transactions remotely, ideal for on-the-go businesses, such as delivery services or market vendors, leveraging mobile connectivity for convenience.</p></p>
