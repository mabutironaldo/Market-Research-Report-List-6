<p><p>Melanoma cancer diagnostics market includes various types of tests such as Fluorescent In Situ Hybridization (FISH) Tests, Comparative Genomic Hybridization (CGH) Tests, and Immunohistochemical (IHC) Tests. FISH tests involve detecting specific DNA sequences through fluorescent labeling, while CGH tests analyze DNA copy number variations. IHC tests use antibodies to detect specific proteins in tissue samples. These diagnostic tests help in accurately diagnosing and determining the prognosis of melanoma, aiding in effective treatment decisions.</p></p>
