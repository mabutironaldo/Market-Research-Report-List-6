<p><p>The bio jet fuel market includes various types, primarily Hydrogenated Vegetable Oil (HVO) and Fischer-Tropsch (FT) fuels. HVO is produced by hydrotreating vegetable oils and animal fats, resulting in a renewable, drop-in fuel that meets aviation standards. Fischer-Tropsch fuel, derived from gasifying biomass or waste feedstocks, creates long-chain hydrocarbons through catalytic processes. Both types offer sustainable alternatives to conventional jet fuel, helping reduce greenhouse gas emissions and reliance on fossil fuels in aviation, while enhancing energy security.</p></p>
