<p><p>The ceramic additive market encompasses various types, including dispersants, lubricants, grinding aids, and others. Dispersants enhance the flow and distribution of ceramic particles, improving uniformity. Lubricants reduce friction during processing, facilitating smoother operations and enhancing product durability. Grinding aids optimize the milling process, increasing efficiency and yield while minimizing energy consumption. Other additives may include binders and deflocculants, which serve specialized roles in enhancing the performance and characteristics of ceramic materials across different applications.</p></p>
