<p><p>The Fixed Displacement Vane Pump market comprises three main types based on displacement capacity: Single Displacement, Double Displacement, and Triple Displacement. Single Displacement pumps have one set of vanes for simple applications, while Double Displacement pumps feature two sets, allowing for higher efficiency and flow rates. Triple Displacement pumps further enhance performance with three sets of vanes, catering to more demanding applications. Each type serves specific industrial needs, with varying levels of pressure output and fluid handling capabilities, influencing market demand and application suitability.</p></p>
