<p><p>Building Integrated Photovoltaic Skylights are innovative solutions that combine traditional skylights with solar panels to generate electricity while providing natural light. There are two main types of panels used in these skylights: Crystalline Panel and Thin Film Panel. Crystalline Panels are made of silicon and offer higher efficiency and longer lifespans, making them more common in the market. Thin Film Panels, on the other hand, are lighter and more flexible, allowing for easier integration into building structures. Both panel types contribute to the growing popularity of solar energy in architectural design.</p></p>
