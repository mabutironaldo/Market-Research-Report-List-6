<p><p>The resorbable adhesion barrier market comprises three primary formulations: film, gel, and liquid. Film formulations are thin sheets that create a protective layer, minimizing adhesion formation during healing. Gel formulations offer a viscous consistency that conforms to tissue surfaces, promoting effective coverage and reducing scar tissue. Liquid formulations provide a free-flowing medium, easily applied to surgical sites, forming a barrier upon contact. Each formulation serves to enhance surgical outcomes by preventing adhesions while being gradually absorbed by the body.</p></p>
