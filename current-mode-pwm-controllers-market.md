<p><p>Current Mode PWM (Pulse Width Modulation) controllers are essential in power supply design, categorized into two main types: AC-DC and DC-DC. AC-DC current mode PWM controllers convert alternating current to direct current, often used in power adapters and chargers. They ensure stable output voltage and reduce electromagnetic interference. In contrast, DC-DC current mode PWM controllers manage the conversion between different DC voltage levels, vital in battery management systems and renewable energy applications, offering efficiency and precise output regulation.</p></p>
