<p><strong>The Linear Displacement Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magnetostrictive Displacement</li><li>Conductive Plastic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Linear displacement sensors are vital for measuring position, motion, and displacement in various applications. Magnetostrictive displacement sensors utilize the magnetostrictive effect to provide precise measurements, often featuring non-contact operation and high accuracy. Conductive plastic sensors, on the other hand, rely on a resistive element made of conductive plastic that changes resistance with displacement. Both types cater to different industrial needs, with magnetostrictive sensors excelling in harsh environments while conductive plastic sensors offer cost-effectiveness and are suitable for less demanding applications.</p></p>
