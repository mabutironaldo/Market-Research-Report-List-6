<p><p>A vapor canister, also known as an evaporative emission canister, is a component in vehicles designed to capture and store fuel vapors from the fuel tank, preventing them from escaping into the atmosphere. It plays a critical role in reducing harmful emissions and ensuring compliance with environmental regulations. The canister stores these vapors and releases them back into the engine for combustion, improving fuel efficiency and reducing pollution.</p><p>The Vapor Canister Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5% during the forecast period. Additionally, technological advancements in vapor canister materials and designs are enhancing their efficiency and durability, further propelling market growth.</p><p>Key trends in the market include the development of lightweight and compact canisters to meet the demands of modern vehicle designs, as well as the integration of smart sensors for real-time monitoring of emission levels.
