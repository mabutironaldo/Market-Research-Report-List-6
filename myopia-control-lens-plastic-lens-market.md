<p><p>The myopia control lens market primarily includes two types: myopia control spectacle lenses and orthokeratology lenses. Myopia control spectacle lenses are specially designed to reduce the progression of nearsightedness in children and young adults, using advanced optical designs. In contrast, orthokeratology lenses are gas-permeable rigid contact lenses worn overnight to reshape the cornea, temporarily correcting myopia. Both types aim to manage and slow down the escalation of myopia, improving visual outcomes and reducing the need for stronger prescriptions.</p></p>
