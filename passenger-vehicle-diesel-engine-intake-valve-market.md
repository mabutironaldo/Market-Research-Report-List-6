<p><p>The passenger vehicle diesel engine intake valve market comprises three primary types: mono metallic, bimetallic, and hollow valves. Mono metallic valves are made from a single metal, providing durability and ease of manufacturing. Bimetallic valves combine two different metals, enhancing thermal resistance and strength. Hollow valves are lightweight with a hollow design that reduces overall weight while maintaining structural integrity. Each type serves specific engine requirements, influencing performance, fuel efficiency, and emissions, ultimately impacting vehicle efficiency and longevity.</p></p>
