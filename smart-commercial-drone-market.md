<p><p>The smart commercial drone market is primarily divided into two types: fixed-wing and rotary blade drones. Fixed-wing drones resemble traditional aircraft, offering efficient long-range flight and stability, making them ideal for applications like agriculture, surveying, and environmental monitoring. In contrast, rotary blade drones, commonly known as multirotors, provide superior maneuverability and vertical takeoff and landing capabilities, suitable for close-range tasks such as aerial photography, inspections, and delivery services. Both types cater to diverse industry needs and operational requirements.</p></p>
