<p><p>Attachment chains are specialized chains used in various industries, categorized primarily into single-pitch and double-pitch types. Single-pitch attachment chains feature links that are connected in a single-pitch arrangement, allowing for precise movement and compatibility with standard sprockets. Conversely, double-pitch attachment chains have links spaced further apart, providing enhanced strength and load capacity, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. Both types are essential in conveying systems, manufacturing, and automation, serving distinct functions based on specific operational needs.</p></p>
