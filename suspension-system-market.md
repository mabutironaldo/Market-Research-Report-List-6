<p><p>The suspension system market includes various types designed to enhance vehicle comfort and stability. **Air Suspension System** uses compressed air to adjust ride height and stiffness, offering a smooth ride. **Hydraulic Suspension System** relies on fluid pressure for damping and load support, providing robust performance. **Electromagnetic Suspension System** employs magnetic fields for rapid response and precise control, improving handling. **Electro-hydraulic Suspension System** combines hydraulic and electronic elements for adaptive damping, balancing comfort and performance. Each system caters to specific vehicle needs and driving conditions.</p></p>
