<p><ul><li>Flangedless</li><li>Flanged</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Soft seal butterfly valves are categorized mainly into flanged and flangeless types. Flanged butterfly valves feature a flange connection, allowing for a secure fit with piping systems, suitable for high-pressure applications. They provide ease of installation and maintenance. In contrast, flangeless butterfly valves are designed without flanges, enabling a compact design and direct mounting to the pipe, thus ideal for space-restricted areas. Both types offer reliable sealing mechanisms and are used in various industries for flow regulation and control.</p></p>
