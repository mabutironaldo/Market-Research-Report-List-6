<p><p>Digital car keys utilize wireless technologies for secure vehicle access. **BLE (Bluetooth Low Energy) Keys** use Bluetooth for proximity-based unlocking, offering convenience and energy efficiency. **NFC (Near Field Communication) Keys** rely on short-range communication, enabling tap-to-unlock functionality with smartphones or cards. **UWB (Ultra-Wideband) Keys** provide precise location tracking and secure access, ideal for advanced features like hands-free entry. Each type caters to different user needs, balancing security, range, and ease of use, driving innovation in the digital car key market.</p></p>
