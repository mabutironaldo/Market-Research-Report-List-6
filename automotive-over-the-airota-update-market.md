<p><ul><li>Firmware over-the-air (FOTA)</li><li>Software over-the-air (SOTA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Over-The-Air (OTA) update market encompasses two primary types: Firmware Over-The-Air (FOTA) and Software Over-The-Air (SOTA). FOTA focuses on updating the vehicle's firmware, which controls essential hardware functions like engine management and safety systems, ensuring optimal performance and compliance with regulations. SOTA, on the other hand, pertains to the updating of software applications that enhance user experience, infotainment systems, and vehicle connectivity features, allowing manufacturers to improve functionality and security remotely without requiring physical service visits.</p></p>
