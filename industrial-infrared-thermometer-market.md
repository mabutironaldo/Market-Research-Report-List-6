<p><ul><li>Contact type</li><li>Contactless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial infrared thermometer market is primarily divided into two types: contact and contactless thermometers. Contact-type thermometers require direct contact with the object to measure temperature, providing accurate readings but limiting their use to stationary or accessible items. In contrast, contactless thermometers, using infrared technology, measure temperature from a distance, enabling rapid assessments without affecting the object's state. This feature makes contactless models ideal for moving targets or hazardous environments, enhancing safety and efficiency in various industrial applications.</p></p>
