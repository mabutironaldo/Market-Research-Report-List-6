<p><p>Inductive proximity sensors detect metal objects without physical contact and are essential in automation and industrial applications. The market primarily features two types: NPN and PNP sensors. NPN sensors operate by providing a ground connection when activated, suitable for sinking applications, while PNP sensors supply a positive voltage, ideal for sourcing applications. These variations enable compatibility with different control systems, allowing manufacturers to select sensors based on their specific circuit requirements and integration needs in automation setups.</p></p>
