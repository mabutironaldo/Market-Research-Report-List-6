<p><p>A self-priming pump is a type of pump designed to remove air from the system and establish a vacuum to draw liquid into the pump without requiring manual intervention. These pumps are commonly used in agricultural, industrial, and municipal applications, including irrigation, sewage treatment, and water supply systems.</p><p>The self-priming pump market is poised for notable growth, driven by increased demand for efficient fluid handling systems across various industries.
