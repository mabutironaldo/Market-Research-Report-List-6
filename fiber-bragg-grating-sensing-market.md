<p><p>Fiber Bragg Grating (FBG) sensing is a technology that utilizes the inherent properties of optical fibers to measure various physical parameters such as temperature, strain, and pressure. FBGs are created by inscribing a periodic pattern of refractive index changes within the fiber, allowing them to reflect specific wavelengths of light while transmitting others. This unique characteristic enables FBG sensors to provide precise and real-time measurements in challenging environments, making them invaluable in sectors like civil engineering, aerospace, and energy.</p><p>The Fiber Bragg Grating Sensing Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for advanced sensing solutions in industrial applications and infrastructure monitoring.
