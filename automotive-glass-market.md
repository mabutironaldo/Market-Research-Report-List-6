<p><ul><li>Laminated Glass</li><li>Tempered Glass</li><li>Special Function Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive glass market comprises three main types: laminated glass, tempered glass, and special function glass. Laminated glass consists of two or more layers bonded with a polymer interlayer, enhancing safety and minimizing injury during accidents. Tempered glass is heat-treated for increased strength and shatter resistance, commonly used in side and rear windows. Special function glass includes advanced features such as acoustic insulation, UV protection, and privacy enhancements, providing added comfort and safety to vehicle occupants.</p></p>
