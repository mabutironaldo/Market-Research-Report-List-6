<p><p>Online booking platforms can be categorized into two main market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise solutions require businesses to install software on their local servers, offering greater control and customization but demanding more maintenance and upfront costs. Conversely, cloud-based platforms operate over the internet, providing scalability and accessibility from any device without heavy upfront investments. This model allows users to benefit from regular updates and reduced IT responsibilities, making it an appealing choice for many modern businesses.</p></p>
