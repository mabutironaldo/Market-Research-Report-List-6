<p><p>The LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene) stretch film market is categorized into two main types: hand film and machine film. Hand film is designed for manual application, offering portability and ease of use for wrapping smaller or irregularly shaped items. In contrast, machine film is engineered for high-volume, automated processes, providing consistent tension and durability during wrapping on palletizing machines. Both types play crucial roles in packaging, ensuring protection and containment of products during storage and transportation.</p></p>
