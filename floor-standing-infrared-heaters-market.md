<p><p>Floor-standing infrared heaters can be categorized into three types based on their wavelength capabilities: far, medium, and near infrared. Far infrared heaters emit longer wavelengths, promoting deep tissue warmth and efficient heating for larger spaces. Medium infrared heaters provide balanced heating, suitable for both indoor and outdoor environments, often offering a comfortable atmosphere. Near infrared heaters, with shorter wavelengths, deliver immediate warmth and are often used for spot heating, ideal for personal or localized comfort.</p></p>
