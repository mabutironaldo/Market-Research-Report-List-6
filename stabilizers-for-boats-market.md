<p><ul><li>Fins Stabilizers</li><li>Gyroscopic Stabilizers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The boat stabilizer market comprises primarily two types: fins stabilizers and gyroscopic stabilizers. Fins stabilizers utilize retractable fins that extend into the water to counteract rolling motions, enhancing stability during navigation. They offer effective stabilization while maintaining speed. In contrast, gyroscopic stabilizers rely on spinning gyroscopes to create angular momentum, providing a smooth ride by counteracting the boat's movements. Both types aim to improve comfort and safety on the water, catering to various boating needs and preferences.</p></p>
