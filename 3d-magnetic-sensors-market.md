<p><p>The 3D Magnetic Sensors market comprises two main types: Linear 3D Magnetic Sensors and Rotary 3D Magnetic Sensors. Linear 3D Magnetic Sensors measure magnetic fields across three dimensions, enabling applications like position detection and linear movement tracking. On the other hand, Rotary 3D Magnetic Sensors are designed to detect angular positions by measuring rotational movements in three-dimensional space, making them ideal for uses in automotive steering systems and robotics. Both types facilitate advancements in automation and precision control across various industries.</p></p>
