<p><p>The IGY polyclonal antibodies market is categorized into primary and secondary antibody segments. Primary antibodies are directly used to detect specific antigens, providing the initial binding in a diagnostic or research setting. In contrast, secondary antibodies recognize and bind to primary antibodies, amplifying the detection signal and enhancing assay sensitivity.
