<p><ul><li>Radio Antenna</li><li>Smart Antenna</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The antenna market can be broadly categorized into two types: radio antennas and smart antennas. Radio antennas are primarily used for traditional broadcasting and communication, enabling long-range transmission for radio signals. In contrast, smart antennas incorporate advanced technologies like beamforming and multiple input/output (MIMO) to optimize signal quality and coverage, making them ideal for applications in wireless networks and mobile communications. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing connectivity and performance across various industries, including telecommunications and broadcasting.</p></p>
