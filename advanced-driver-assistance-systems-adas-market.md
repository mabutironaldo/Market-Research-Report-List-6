Lane Departure Warning (LDW) uses sensors to alert drivers if they unintentionally drift out of their lane. Park Assist facilitates easier parking by guiding drivers into parking spots. Blind Spot Detection monitors areas outside the driver's view, warning of vehicles nearby. Other systems encompass features like forward collision warning and automatic emergency braking.</p></p>
