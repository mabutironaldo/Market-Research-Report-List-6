<p><strong>The Chilled Products Transport Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vapor Compression Systems</li><li>Cryogenic Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chilled products transport market includes two main types of refrigeration systems: Vapor Compression Systems and Cryogenic Systems. Vapor Compression Systems use a refrigerant to cool the air inside the transport container. These systems are more common and cost-effective for smaller-scale operations. On the other hand, Cryogenic Systems use liquid nitrogen or carbon dioxide to maintain ultra-low temperatures. These systems are typically used for transporting highly perishable goods over long distances. Both systems play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and safety of chilled products during transportation.</p></p>
