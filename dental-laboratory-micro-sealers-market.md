<p><p>The dental laboratory micro-sealers market comprises three main types: laser, infrared, and pulse arc sealers. Laser micro-sealers employ focused light technology to achieve precise sealing, promoting strong adhesion and minimal heat transfer. Infrared sealers utilize infrared radiation to heat materials for effective sealing, ensuring uniform application. Pulse arc sealers operate using short bursts of energy, combining heat and pressure for effective bonding. Each type offers unique advantages, enhancing the efficiency and quality of dental restorations and prosthetics.</p></p>
