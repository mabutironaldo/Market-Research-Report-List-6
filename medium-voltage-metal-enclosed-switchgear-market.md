<p><p>The medium voltage metal-enclosed switchgear market is segmented into three main types. Air Insulated Metal-Enclosed Switchgear (AISM) utilizes air for insulation and is suitable for outdoor applications. Gas Insulated Metal-Enclosed Switchgear (GIS) employs gas insulation, providing a compact solution ideal for space-constrained environments. Solid Insulated Metal-Enclosed Switchgear (SIMS) employs solid insulation materials, offering enhanced safety and environmental protection. Each type caters to specific operational needs, emphasizing reliability and efficiency in power distribution systems.</p></p>
