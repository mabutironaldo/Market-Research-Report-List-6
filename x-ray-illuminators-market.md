<p><ul><li>Multi-Layer Coating Optical System</li><li>Single-Layer Coating Optical System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>X-ray illuminators feature two primary market types based on their optical coating systems: multi-layer coating optical systems and single-layer coating optical systems. Multi-layer coating systems utilize several layers of coatings to enhance light transmission and minimize reflection, resulting in improved image quality and contrast. In contrast, single-layer coating systems consist of a single coating layer, offering a simpler and typically less expensive solution but with reduced optical performance. Both types are essential in various applications, including medical imaging and industrial inspection.</p></p>
