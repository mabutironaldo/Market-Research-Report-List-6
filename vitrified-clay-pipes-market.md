<p><strong>The Vitrified Clay Pipes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Trench Socket Pipes</li><li>Microtunneling (Jacking) Vitrified Clay Pipes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vitrified clay pipes market consists of two primary types: open trench socket pipes and microtunneling (jacking) vitrified clay pipes. Open trench socket pipes are installed using traditional excavation methods, providing reliable, gravity-flow systems for wastewater management. In contrast, microtunneling vitrified clay pipes are installed using advanced tunneling techniques, minimizing surface disruption and allowing for installations in constrained environments. Both types offer durability, chemical resistance, and sustainability, making them ideal for various civil engineering applications in infrastructure projects.</p></p>
