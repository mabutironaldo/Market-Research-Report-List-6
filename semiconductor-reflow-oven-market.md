<p><strong>The Semiconductor Reflow Oven Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Convection Ovens</li><li>Vapour Phase Ovens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor reflow oven market primarily includes convection ovens and vapor phase ovens. Convection ovens use heated air to uniformly transfer heat to the circuit boards, ensuring even soldering of components. They offer precise temperature control and are popular in high-volume production. In contrast, vapor phase ovens utilize a vaporized liquid to transfer heat, providing a gentler process that reduces thermal stress on sensitive components. Both types cater to different manufacturing needs, balancing speed, efficiency, and component protection.</p></p>
